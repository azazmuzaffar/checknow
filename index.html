About

what drives us

21 Impact Labs is reimagining impact funding for the 21st century by scaling communities, talent, and capital globally to advance brain longevity, and accelerate solutions and cures to Alzheimer’s disease.

Through the use of crypto economic mechanisms and Web3 technologies we will make impact funding more open, transparent, scalable, and democratic.

We fundamentally believe that properly designed crypto economic mechanisms are the most powerful way to organize talent and capital on a global scale to pursue a common mission.
